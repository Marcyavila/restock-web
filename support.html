Frequently asked questions

What is ReStock Pro?
ReStock Pro is a Chrome extension that monitors product pages (e.g. at Target and other stores) and notifies you when items restock or become available for preorder, so you don’t have to keep checking manually.
How do I get early access?
Join the waitlist on our homepage with your email. We’ll send one email when the extension is available on the Chrome Web Store. Early users get lifetime perks.
Alerts seem wrong or delayed—why?
We rely on how stores display their pages. If a site changes its layout or blocks automated checks, alerts can be delayed or incorrect. We’re always improving. If you see a specific product or store that’s off, tell us so we can look into it.
Which stores are supported?
We currently support Target and are working on more. If you have a store you’d like us to add, email us—we use that feedback to prioritize.
