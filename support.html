What does ReStock Pro do?

ReStock Pro is a Chrome extension that monitors product pages (e.g. at Target and other stores) and notifies you when items restock or become available for preorder, so you don’t have to keep checking manually.

How often does ReStock Pro check for restocks?

ReStock Pro checks selected product pages periodically in the background. The frequency may vary depending on store restrictions, rate limits, and system load.

Why am I not getting restock alerts?

Make sure notifications are enabled in Chrome and that the extension is allowed to show alerts. Also verify your browser is running and the extension is enabled.

If you’re still not seeing alerts, email us with the product link you’re tracking and what you expected vs what happened.

The extension icon disappeared — what happened?

Open Chrome → Extensions → Manage Extensions. Find ReStock Pro and ensure it is enabled.

How do I get early access?

Join the waitlist on our homepage with your email. We’ll send one email when the extension is available on the Chrome Web Store.

Alerts seem wrong or delayed—why?

We rely on how stores display their pages. If a site changes its layout or blocks automated checks, alerts can be delayed or incorrect. If alerts stop working for a specific store, it usually means the retailer updated their website. Send us the product link and we’ll investigate.

Which stores are supported?

We currently support Target and are working on more. If you have a store you’d like us to add, email us—we use that feedback to prioritize.

Can I request a store or feature?

Yes. Send us the store name and a product link (or a few examples). We prioritize additions based on user demand and feasibility.
